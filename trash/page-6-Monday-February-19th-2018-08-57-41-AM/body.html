Prologue

On the surface writing a book may seem very different from app development—they are different trades after all—but at the heart of each, in the steps of their reification, there are more similarities than differences. In fact there is hardly any difference at all! For writers, of both books or software, it is ultimately the same run towards getting to a great story, a seductive design, engagement, skilled packaging and an impeccable launch.

A magical product.

Comparing a day in life of an author with that of a developer we can quickly arrive at how closely their life traits are related and how tooling for one leaves a lot to ask for while for the other it is just too much to take in. Each trade offers a wide selection of opportunities, a set of daring challenges and a series of goalposts that must be must be met.

Line by line, edit after edit both software writers and book authors try to find a cathartic meaning to their lives—or simply earn money or to add to their careers. Both products undergo distribution challenges to their creators and lean on professional help on the project.
