Device Support

A blank superbook is suppoted on all devices (all viewports) and all desktops (all operating systems) with a modern browser. It is only the content that would eventually limit the outreach of your book.

For example, webgl may not be supported on some devices and older desktops. If your book uses webgl, then it will certainly not be available on those devices. Use responsive typography for text and images to compose the pages of your book.

Always test your content i.e. HTML, CSS and JavaScript on multiple devices and look for support and known issues on caniuse. Else you'll risk supporting fewer devices than you possibly could.

Pro tip: Providing support information on the cover page of your book is a great help to your potential readers.
