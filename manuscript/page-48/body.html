HEAD: CDN Resources

Ability to apply and include CDN resources into your superbook is one of the best features of Bubblin. It's an outcome of bringing up web & books together.

There are two ways to apply CDN resources into the HEAD of your page:

  1. via templates, or
  2. via per-page HEAD component.

Templates are an ideal way to configure the HEAD of your entire book. For example, if you're writing a book on mathematics or on elementary particle physics then it is likely that you're going to need Mathjax (Latex / Katex) on all of your pages.

Use templates for that.
