HEAD: CDN Resources

There are two ways to apply CDN resources into the HEAD of your page:

  1. Via the HEAD template,
  2. Per page HEAD includes.

Templates are an ideal way to configure the HEAD of your entire book. If you're writing a book for say higher secondary mathematics or elementary particle physics, then it is probably a good idea to configure Mathjax (Latex / Katex) as a default on all your pages.

Paste the CDN_url into the head overlay or template as show below.

Note, it is mandatory to use https for all resources that are included into your book.
