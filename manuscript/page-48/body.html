Table of Contents

It is very easy and important to add a Table of Contents to your book, especially if it is really long. Table of Contents provides critical accessibility deep inside your book and helps a reader to jump from one page on your book to another easily.

Table of Contents can be submitted via a textarea that accepts HTML as an unordered list alongwith a className of toc like so: 

<ul class="toc">
  <!--- List items here --->
</ul>

Feel free to use the _table_of_contents_ of this handbook itself (under MIT License ) to create a table of contents for your own.

Automatic Generation

Bookiza combined with the Abelone tool can generate Table of Contents automatically. Discussion about how that works is kind of out of scope for this book.
