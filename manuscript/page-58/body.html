Pricing Tools

Let's take a look at pricing tools now. By default a new Superbook is unpriced and unmarked i.e. unlocked. When a book is published without a price and a marker, all of its pages are available for anyone to view & share for free.

To set a price go to your book in editing mode and simply add Price Point in cents (US) at which you wish to sell your book, via the overlay (shown) on the Pricing tab.
