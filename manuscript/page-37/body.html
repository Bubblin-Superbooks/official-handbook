Leafs & Pages

Adding pages to a book is very easy but also a unique feature on Bubblin. Before we get into it, it is important to understand the following truth about any and every book out there:

All books have even number of pages!

No matter how thick or thin the book (or magazine) is, no matter how many leaves it has, a book will always have even number of pages. Consider this: each leaf has two sides — a front & a back — and therefore a book with 15 leafs will have 30 pages.

Likewise on Bubblin a leaf is always two-sided with an edge that binds into the spine (bifloium shown overleaf) of the superbook.
