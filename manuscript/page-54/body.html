Cover Design

Designing a cover on Bubblin is very easy. Use our PSD cover and spine templates to design the top, bottom and spine of your book. These are available under MIT License, feel free to use and redistribute.

Always save_image_for_the_web and optimize your PNG/JPG image using compressors like OptiPNG or jpegTrans. To change the cover of your book simply replace simply by reuploading a new image.
