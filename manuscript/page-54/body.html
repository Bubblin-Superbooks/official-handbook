Selling your book

Status: This feature is available only by invitation.

Helping writers make money off their work is an important goal of Bubblin. We provide a simple set of tools to price and sell your book easily.

With a freedom unlike anywhere else.

There are three steps to follow:

  1. Choose a price point for your book,
  2. Determine a sales model,
  3. View the royalty structure (if applicable).

… and done. Your book is ready to go!

One of the big advantage here is that Bubblin lets you choose a price for your book flexibly. According to your readership. You can also choose to publish your work for free! It is totally up to you—the creator—how you want to build your own community of readers. And do well.
