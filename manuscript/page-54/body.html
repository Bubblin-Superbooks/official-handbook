Book Summary

Describe your book in no more than 2-3 lines ( less than 300 alphabets). An ideal summary gives a high level pitch of the book to the reader.

Example:

"A quick and immersive journey through our Solar System with a soft primer on Gravitational Physics. Must read for aspiring astronomers, star-gazers and science enthusiasts of all ages out there. Learn how our planetary system works and how astronomy connects all of science together!"

- The Solar System, M. Danig
