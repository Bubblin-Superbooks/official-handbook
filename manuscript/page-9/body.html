Device Support

A blank Superbook is supported on all devices, all viewports and all desktops (all operating systems) sporting a modern browser.

It is only the content (underlying CSS or frontmatter) that could limit the outreach of your book. For example, webgl may not be supported on some of the older devices or desktops. If your book uses webgl, then it will not be available on those devices.

Pro tip: Use viewport units (CSS) for responsive typography like the one described on this template to support as many devices as possible.

Pro tip: Refer caniuse for support of HTML5 & CSS3 features to provide appropriate information on the cover page of your book.
