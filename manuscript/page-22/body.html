Layouts

Templates are an excellent way to apply a layout (style) all across a book. But then style isn't everything. Sometimes you also need a template for HTML, JavaScript & HEAD to use all along of your book.

On Bubblin website:

On Bookiza, layout files can be placed in the templatesfolder ($ [projectname] > templates/) of your project. Bookiza will automagically pick up the contents and apply it to all the pages of your book.

Check out some free templates on Github to get started.

Contributions are welcome!
