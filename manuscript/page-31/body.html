Page Numbers

It's very easy to add page numbers to a book. Click on the Page numbers option from the Page submenu as shown below and select the checkbox.

Uncheck to remove the page numbers from the book.

Not all books (like comics or photobooks) need page numbers, so this switch is handy to turn page_numbers on or off as it may be necessary. Page numbers are displayed at the top corners of the book as a standard.

This cannot be customized.
