It is pertinent to mention here that Bubblin doesn't consider the history of development (GIT Tree) of your story. It will hold and serve only the HEAD version, i.e. the last published work that was supplied to the production container (Superbook). All previous versions are OVERWRITTEN.

Therefore, a versioning software like GIT or SVN must always be used to retain history elsewhere.
