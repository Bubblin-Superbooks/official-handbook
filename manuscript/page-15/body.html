It is pertinent to mention here that Bubblin doesn't care about the history of the development of your story. Bubblin will hold and serve only the last published work that is supplied to the production container (Superbook). The previous versions are always OVERWRITTEN.

Therefore, you MUST use a versioning software like GIT or SVN to keep history of your work elsewhere.
