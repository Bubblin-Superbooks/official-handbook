Setup

Let's get to setting up Stripe first. You must be logged in to your Bubblin account to intgerate stripe for payments. If you are already working on a new book project simply hover on the Pricing tab on the top of your book (See screengrab below).

Click through Set up Stripe Connect option to initiate stripe integration. If you already have a Stripe Account, simply login and authorize access to Bubblin and you're good. If you're new to Stripe then you're going to need the following pieces of information to complete your setup.
