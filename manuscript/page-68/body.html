Setup

Let's get to setting up Stripe first. You must be logged in to your Bubblin account to intgerate payments. If you are writing/editing a new book simply hover on the Pricing tab on the top of your book (See screengrab below).

Click through Setup Stripe Connect option to initiate integration. If you already have a Stripe Account simply login and authorize access to Bubblin. You're all set.

If you're new to Stripe then you're going to need the following pieces to complete your setup:

  1. A bank account (with routing number)
  2. A working email address
  3. A business address

You'll see a signup form from Stripe (see overleaf).
