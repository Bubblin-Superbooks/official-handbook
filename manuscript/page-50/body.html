Responsible CSS3

On Bubblin you can apply CSS to your books in two ways:

  1. via template for entire book, or
  2. with per-page CSS for finer control.

Before setting to write your first CSS rule, understand that all books must be responsive and scalable as per Bubblin's desired standards for Superbooks. Don't just blindly include reset.css / normalize.css when you're not going to use more than half of its rules.

Refer free templates to help you get started.

CSS is an important aspect of your book. It styles the content and gives your book a personality. Not only that, CSS can also become the content itself. Like with our sleepy parrot to wake you up to CSS3.
