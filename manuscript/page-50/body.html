Responsible CSS3

You can apply CSS in two different ways:

  1. via a template for the entire book, or
  2. with per-page CSS for finer control.

Before writing your first style rule please understand that all books must be responsive & adaptive as per Bubblin's prescribed standards. Do not just blindly add reset/ normalize css or try to emulate layout of a physical book/magazine on the web.

Refer our free template to help you get started on valid viewport-specific responses for your CSS.

CSS has an important function for your book. It styles the content of your book and gives it a personality. But the role of CSS doesn't stop there. CSS can also become the content itself. Like it did with our sleepy parrot earlier.
