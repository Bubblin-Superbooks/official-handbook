Bookiza will watch for changes as you write your story. Open the project on your favorite editor (Visual Studio, Sublime or Atom) and start building your story.

Once it is ready, hit:

$ bookiza publish

Boom!, your book will be relayed into your Bubblin (Open '/drafts' on Bubblin to see) account. It can go live now for the world to read.

Complete bookiza vocabulary is available with:

$ bookiza --help

Learn more about Bookiza on its documentation page.
