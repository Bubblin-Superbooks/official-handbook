Internal Links & Selectability

Internal links:

Internal links of the book are handled differently from the links that lead to webpages outside the book. Use class="page" href="[page_no]" to link to pages within the book. Checkout the HTML of the index page of this book, for example.

Selectability

Sometimes, expecially on the desktops, people want to be able to select text on the page or inside an element. Like so:

$ bookiza update
Use class="turnoff" to prevent page flip and allow selectability of the text of an element. Cool!
