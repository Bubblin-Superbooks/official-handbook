Eventually, web will catch up and adapt to the new landscape of devices; likely outrun the closed gardens that it inspires from. But if we're to believe and infer from history, then scrolls of texts and inscriptions were replaced by boundbooks forever. That old must give way to the new, so as to facilitate reading on mobile and touch devices.

With this flurry of thoughts I welcome you to our Official Handbook on writing superbooks. Feel free to write to me or follow me on Twitter if you like.

- Marvin Danig, September 2016
