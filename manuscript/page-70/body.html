Stripe Connect

Connecting Stripe to your Bubblin account is easy. You must be logged in to your Bubblin account to do so. Now, if you're already writing a new book at the moment, simply hover on the Pricing tab on the top of the editor.

Click the Set up Stripe Connect option and complete the process. You're going to need the following to open a new Stripe Connect account if you don't have one already:

  1. A bank account (routing number),
  2. A working email address,
  3. A business address.

If you have a Stripe account simply authorize payments via Bubblin and you're all set.
