Introduction

Bubblin is a world-class open and decentralized storytelling substrate for writers and artists. Publish for the world to enjoy your stories, build your own fan following of readers, make loads of money and avoid middlemen (including us) completely.

Our platform and polyfill for Superbooks work on all devices, viewports and operating systems sporting a modern browser. It comes pre-integrated with Bookiza a low-level development toolchain for e-books.
