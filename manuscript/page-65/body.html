Colophon

Colophons can be added directly into a page of the book. It is likely that no separate functionality will be provided for colophons, for not all books beg one, but there is a thought that colophons can be extracted into the Cover Page of a book so that writer can provide a brief statement about the publication of the book such as the place of publication, the publisher, and the date of publication.

What are your thoughts? Share your feedback with us.

Status: Feature under discussion.
