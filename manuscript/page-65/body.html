Book Reviews

It's likely that Bubblin will remain free of all noise and signals of ecommerce — commentary, product reviews and opinionated responses for the reason that design language of Bubblin has been totally "designless" so far. Our focus is solely on the body of creation, i.e. reading & writing books. Promote a kind of silence in which books can thrive and enjoyed genuinely.

Anything that relates to commerce or socializing — such as putting in reviews, adding comments — should perhaps happen elsewhere, independently, after a book has been read and needs to be written about. A word in the wild is likely more authentic (and counterable?) and doesn't need to sit close to the body of creation itself.

What are your thoughts? Share your feedback with us.
