Templates

Templates are a great way to apply uniform style, js or default html on Superbooks. They're an incredibly powerful tool on Bubblin. Imagine applying a piece of code across hundreds of pages in just one click.

Check out some of the free templates available under MIT license to style and get started on your book right away. Feel free to fork & tweak any of those themes according to your requirements. Contribute!

On Bookiza projects templates can be applied inside the Templatesfolder (go to $projectname > templates/) and save the template to its corresponding file. Bookiza will automagically pick up the contents and apply it to all the pages of your book.
