Add / Remove Pages

Adding pages to a book on Bubblin is very easy but a unique concept. It is important to understand something here that is true for any book out there:

All books have even number of pages.

No matter how thick or thin the book (or magazine) is, no matter how many leaves it has, a book will always have even number of pages. Consider this: each leaf has two sides — front & back — and therefore a book with say 15 leaves will have 30 pages, counting both sides in.

Leaves are also generally folded into a bifolium, with an edge that binds to the spine (shown overleaf) of the book. On Bubblin we consider adding pages akin to adding one leaf at a time i.e. two sides/pages.
