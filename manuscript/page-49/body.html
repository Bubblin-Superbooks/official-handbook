Cover Page

People judge a book by its cover. Or let's say by its Cover Page and Summary. Cover page is the landing page of your book (See screenshots overleaf). This has several elements to tell a quick story about your book. And proposition it if you want to sell it online.

Bubblin provides several widgets to make your Cover Page compelling. There are a few mandatory fields on this page which must be complete at the time of publishing your book.
