Settings page

You can also connect Stripe from the Settings page. Click on the Stripe tab (shown below) and click the Connect Stripe button to get you started.

Managing Sales & Refunds:

Note that all payments, transactions, charges, deposits and chargebacks are managed by Stripe. Make sure you've read up Stripe's documentation, terms and policy on how to use Stripe Connect dashboard to track and manage your sales.

All transactions are in US dollars.
