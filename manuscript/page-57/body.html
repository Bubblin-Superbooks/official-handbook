Settings page

You can also connect Stripe via the Settings page. Click on the APIs tab (shown) and then click 'Connect Stripe' button to enable payments.

Managing Sales & Refunds:

Note that all payments, transactions, charges, deposits and chargebacks are managed by Stripe. Make sure you've read Stripe's documentation, terms and policy and how to use the Stripe Connect dashboard to track and manage your sales.

All transactions are in US dollars only.
