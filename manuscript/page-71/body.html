Book price

By default a new book is always unpriced and unmarked. If such a book is published all of its pages will be available for anyone to read for free. To price your book simply set a book_price (in cents) via the overlay. The minimum you can sell your book for is 51ï¿ .

Note that setting a price doesn't lock down your book behind a paywall. It merely says that your book is priced. This means that you can price a book and still keep it open for everyone to read. In a hope that some of those readers will eventually pay you for the work.
