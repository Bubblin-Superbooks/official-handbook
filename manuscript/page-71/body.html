Pricing Tools

Once Stripe Connect is integrated with your Bubblin Account, you'll see the following three vectors to help you choose a price and a sales model for your superbook:

  1. Price in cents
  2. Page marker
  3. Commission (Optional)

Note, all transactions are in US dollars only. And Stripe is currently available only in the following countries.
