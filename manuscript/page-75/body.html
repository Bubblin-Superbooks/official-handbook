Commissions / Royalty

Third most important arrangement related to pricing and sales is the zero commission guarantee from the Bubblin Foundation. We are a non-profit organization and we do not take a cut from the sales/profit our authors or artists make.

We rely on grants & donations instead.

How to donate?

You can choose to donate a percentage of your book sales by using the Commission option on the Pricing tab or give a one time donation on our website if you like.

Donations help us cover the server, hosting and other costs for you and your readers so please donate generously.
