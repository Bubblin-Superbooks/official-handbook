Commission

Third most important featurette pertaining to pricing and sales is the zero commission guarantee from the Bubblin Foundation. We are a non-profit organization and we do not cut into the sales/profit our authors and artists make on their books.

We rely on donations instead. Do read our manifesto to learn more about our story and why we even exist.

How to donate?

You can choose to donate a percentage of your book sales by using the Commission option on the Pricing tab or give a one time donation if you like.

Donations help us cover the server, hosting and other costs for you and your readers so please donate generously to help us keep running.

Info: The Bubblin Foundation is a Registered Non-Profit in the State of Virginia, USA.
