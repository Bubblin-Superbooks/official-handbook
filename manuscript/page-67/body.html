Sales

Coming to the most important aspect about professional book writing — sales & profit — let's take a quick look at the options and flexibility provided by Bubblin first.

Bubblin lets the author decide price of their books. We do not intervene, hedge or recommend pricing (or worth) for your book. Our philosophy is that if it is useful and priced correctly people are happy to pay.
