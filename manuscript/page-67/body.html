Sales

Bubblin provides a simple set of tools to price and sell your work online. And a freedom unlike anywhere else.

Here's a quick glance on how it works:

  1. Set a price for your book,
  2. Decide a sales model for it,
  3. Set a royalty -- if your wish to -- for Bubblin.

That's it.

Your book is ready to go online and make money.

One of the biggest advantage of Bubblin here is that it lets you price your book flexibly. Unlike other e-book distribution platforms we do not influence or regulate the price of your work at any stage.
