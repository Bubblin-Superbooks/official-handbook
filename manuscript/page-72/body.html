Stripe Connect

Setting up Stripe Connect on your Bubblin account is very easy. You must be logged in to your Bubblin account to do so.

If you are writing a book simply hover on the Pricing tab on the top of your book (See below).

Click into Setup Stripe Connect option and follow through the steps shown next. If you already have a Stripe account simply authorize payments via Bubblin and you're all set.

If you're new to Stripe Connect here's a list of things you're going to need:

  1. A bank account (with routing number)
  2. A working email address
  3. A business address
