Stripe Connect

Bubblin uses Stripe Connect under the hood. It is very easy to connect your Bubblin account with Stripe.

First, you must be logged in to your Bubblin account to do so. Now, if you're already writing a book currently, simply hover on the Pricing tab on the top of the editor.

Click the Set up Stripe Connect option and complete the process at Stripe. You're all set!

Status: This is an alpha-stage feature and may change in future.
