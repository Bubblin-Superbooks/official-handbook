A step-by-step guide on how to write and publish uber cool superbooks. Bubblin is a fresh approach to writing books on the web, it uses a subtle blend of brilliant app development techniques and modern designing concepts to forge together "book-like" single-page apps.

It's so easy that you'll ride a bestseller with less than half-the-effort you'd have to put elsewhere!

Our official handbook itself is created using Bookiza, the tiniest framework for book writing out there. With this handbook help will arrive in every step of the way. It will charge up your book writing days with the experience of writing an app. Hopefully you'll find the handbook very useful and be able to tap into the advantages of using building blocks of web to power your own book. Feel free to share your feedback and suggestions at marvin@bubbl.in.

The source of this superbook is available on Github under MIT license. Fork it or submit pull requests to add / edit content!
