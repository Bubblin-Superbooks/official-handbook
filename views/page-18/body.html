Device Support

By default, a blank superbook is supported on all devices (all viewports) and all desktops (all operating systems) with a modern browser. It is only the content that could limit the outreach of your book.

For example, webgl may not be supported on some Android devices, older iPhones and desktops. In case your book uses webgl, then it will not be available on those devices. It is important to emphasize here that you must use only responsive typography to compose the pages of your book. Else you'll risk supporting even fewer devices than you could.

Always test your content i.e. HTML, CSS and JavaScript on multiple devices and look for support and known issues on caniuse.

Pro tip: Provide complete support information to your readers on the cover page of your book.
